What is a Computer?

A computer is an electronic device that processes data and performs tasks according to a set of instructions called a program. It consists of hardware components such as the central processing unit (CPU), memory, storage devices, input/output devices, and software that enables it to function. Computers can be used for a wide range of applications, including word processing, internet browsing, gaming, and scientific research.
